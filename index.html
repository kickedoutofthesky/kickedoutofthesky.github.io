BIO

Kicked Out Of The Sky is a project that was formulated in 2020 by Matt Bachman. Matt had musical ideas swimming in his head, but was not quite sure of the creative direction. He started recording and creating songs in his kitchen on Logics Pro X, in Burbank CA. Matt’s influences have always revolved around rock/alternative/pop and pop punk. He loves incorporating multiple instruments into his songs including violin, keys, hooky guitar riffs and solid drums. Thus, his own style of pop punk was born. Matt started by recording all instrumentation, producing, mastering on his own.
The first single Matt released in 2020 was titled “Kicked Out Of The Sky” with an accompanying video, shot in his backyard. The second release in 2021 was “Reason To Believe”, with a supporting video filmed on a sound stage.
The latest release, “How I Feel”, and the upcoming EP, “Levity Of Lies”, was an amazing experience as he got the chance to work with one of his favorite producers/engineer/mixers Mike Plotnikoff who has engineered, co-produced albums by POD, Buck Cherry, My Chemical Romance and other big named bands. Matt also had a few amazing musician friends come on board such as Phil X, Glen Sobel, Mimi Pleschet, to help bring his latest musical creations to light. The new single is supported by a really twisted an edgy video.
